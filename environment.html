Prakruti Rakshati Rakshita

Healing Our Planet, Protecting Our Future

No Plastic Usage
Break Free from Plastic

Plastic is choking our planet. From our streets to our oceans, it's a problem we can see everywhere. We are committed to a plastic-free India by promoting simple, everyday swaps. Join us in saying 'no' to single-use plastics like bags, bottles, and straws. Choose reusable, sustainable alternatives and help us protect our land, wildlife, and health for generations to come.

Refuse, Reuse, Recycle.

Tree Plantation
Plant a Tree, Grow a Legacy

Trees are the lungs of our world. They give us clean air, fight climate change, and provide shelter for wildlife. Our mission is to bring life back to India's green cover through large-scale tree plantation drives. We don't just plant trees; we nurture them. We also offer services for communities and businesses to start their own green spaces, because every tree counts in building a healthier, happier India.

One Tree, One Life. Plant More.

Battery Car Usage
Drive Green, Breathe Clean

The future of travel is clean, quiet, and electric! By switching to battery cars (EVs), we can drastically cut down the toxic air pollution from our cities. Electric vehicles are not just a global trend; they are India's key to clearer skies and a quieter, more sustainable future. Discover the benefits of EVs and join the revolution for cleaner commutes and a healthier nation.

Go Electric. For Zero Emissions.

Nature Improvement
Heal Our Planet

Our well-being is directly connected to the health of our natural world. "Nature Improvement" is all about taking active steps to restore and protect our environment. This includes cleaning up our rivers and lakes, protecting wildlife, promoting organic farming, and conserving soil and water. It's a collective effort to heal the damage and ensure that India's incredible natural beauty thrives for our children.

Heal the Damage. Restore the Earth.
