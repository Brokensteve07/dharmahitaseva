Spirit of Selfless Service

Annadānam is the greatest donation –
The Gift of Food is the Supreme Gift

Our Seva programs stand at the core of our trust, representing the spirit of selfless service. We believe that true worship lies in serving humanity. Whether it is feeding the hungry, supporting the elderly, or helping communities in times of difficulty, we strive to be present wherever support is needed. Every act of giving is our sacred offering, carried out with compassion and responsibility toward society.

Annadanam

Regular free food distribution (Bhandara) for individuals and families in need.

Vastra Danam

Providing clothes, blankets, and essential daily-use items to the underprivileged.

Our Core Values

Integrity • Empathy • Sustainability • Accountability

Elderly & Orphan Support: Assistance to old-age homes, orphanages, and shelters for the destitute.

Disaster Relief: Emergency aid, ration supply, and medical support during natural calamities.
